Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our link collection service:

    Our website uses cookies to enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences and improving site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you choose.

    We may update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Continued use of our service after changes indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

If you have any questions regarding these terms or policies, please contact us at hello@keeplinker.com.
